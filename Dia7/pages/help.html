🆘 How to Play – Game Guide

🎮 Starting the Game

Click the "Start" button on the homepage to begin your match.

🂠 The Cards

At the start of each round, two hidden cards will appear on screen (one for each player).

You won’t see their values until you choose to reveal them.

🔁 Changing Your Card

Next to each card, there’s a "Change Card" button.

Click it to swap your card as many times as you want before revealing.

You can change your card multiple times or keep the one you have.

🎴 Winning Cards

After each round, the cards that each player wins will be collected and shown on the side,

between the "Change Card" button and the hidden card section.

These cards are saved until the end to decide the winner!

👁️ Revealing the Cards

Once both players are ready, click the "Show Cards" button.

Both cards will be flipped face-up, revealing their true values.

🏆 Who Wins the Round?

  • The player with the higher card wins the round and takes both cards.
  • Card values:
    • 🃏 J, Q, K = 10 points
    • 🅰️ A (Ace) = 11 points
    • 🔢 Number cards (2–10) = their face value
  • 🤝 If both players have cards with the same value (e.g. a J and a K, both worth 10), it's a tie and no one gets the cards.

🔄 New Rounds

After revealing the cards, click the "Next Round" button to continue.

⚠️ If you don’t click "Next Round", the screen will stay the same — the game won’t move on until you decide to.

👑 How to Win the Game

When the deck runs out, the player with the most collected cards is the winner! 🎉

❌ Exiting the Game

To leave the game at any time, click the "X" button in the top-left corner.
